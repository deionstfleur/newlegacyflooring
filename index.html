About


Legacy Flooring LLC provides superior service in the floor covering industry in a professional and efficient manner while offering quality products at an affordable price. We are committed to maintain a positive work environment that reflects honesty, integrity and fairness when dealing with our clients and staff. We will actively continue to educate ourselves on the newest trends in the flooring industry to better serve our customers. Our goal is to leave a lasting sense of satisfaction for our customers.
